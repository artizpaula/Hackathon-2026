Inspiration
Our goal and inspiration was to personalize travel planning by automatically recommending places based on your mood and available time.

What it does
FlyMood suggests travel spots according to your current feelings. You complete a short emotional quiz and link your calendar, and the app utilizes AI to recommend places that suit your mood and available time.

How we built it
We created it with a Shiny app, combining APIs for accessing calendars, travel information, and suggestions based on AI.

Challenges we ran into
Connecting to the API could be inconsistent at times, and achieving reliable responses took much trial and error along with troubleshooting.

Accomplishments that we're proud of
We linked the app to an individual calendar and automatically identified available time slots to create practical travel options.

What we learned
We discovered how APIs function in practical situations and that keeping things simple in Shiny applications is important. A simpler design results in more stability and a better experience for users.

What's next for FlyMood
We aim to introduce a travel history option that allows users to keep, look back at, and expand upon their previous FlyMood suggestions.
